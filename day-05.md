## What I learned
- Raster images (JPG, PNG) are resolution-dependent and can lose quality when scaled
- SVG images are vector-based and scale without losing quality
- JPG is best for photographs and images with many colors
- PNG supports transparency and is better for graphics and icons
- SVG is ideal for logos, icons, and responsive designs due to infinite scalability
- Image format choice impacts performance, quality, and responsiveness
